#PGWECN!


Welcome to PGWECN! We're a group of Content Creators, YouTubers, Twitch Streamers, TikTokers, gamers and people dedicated to mental health! We believe in helping all those in need, And enjoying the life we have!

Background!

Founded in 2020 by Dr.Keefy(Keith A. Counts), we were made at first to create wholesome content, but we've slowly moved past that into bigger and better things! Our goals slowly moved towards helping people with mental health. Now on both Keefy's Guilded and our Discord, we do tickets, therapy, and even have teams dedicated for it!
